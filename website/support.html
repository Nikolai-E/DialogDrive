Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install DialogDrive?

Click the "Add to Chrome" button on our homepage or visit the Chrome Web Store. Click "Add to Chrome" and confirm the installation. The DialogDrive icon will appear in your extensions toolbar.

How do I capture a chat or prompt?

There are several ways to capture content:

  • Popup/Side Panel: Click the "Save Current Chat" or "New Prompt" button
  • Keyboard Shortcut: Use the configurable shortcuts (check chrome://extensions/shortcuts)
  • Context Menu: Right-click selected text and choose "Save to DialogDrive"

How do I organize my prompts and chats?

DialogDrive offers multiple organization features:

  • Workspaces: Group related prompts and chats together
  • Tags: Add multiple tags to each item for flexible categorization
  • Pinning: Pin important items to the top of your list
  • Search & Filter: Quickly find items by title, content, workspace, or tags

Is my data safe and private?

Yes! DialogDrive stores all your data locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. We don't collect personal information, track your usage, or send data to external servers. See our Privacy Policy for complete details.

Can I export my data?

Yes! DialogDrive includes export functionality. Open the extension popup or side panel, navigate to Settings, and use the "Export Data" option to download all your prompts, chats, and settings as a JSON file.

How do I import data from a backup?

In the Settings section, use the "Import Data" option and select your previously exported JSON file. Your prompts, chats, workspaces, and tags will be restored.

Can I use DialogDrive with AI providers other than ChatGPT?

Currently, DialogDrive is optimized for ChatGPT (chatgpt.com). Support for additional providers like Gemini and Claude is on the roadmap for future releases.

How do I configure keyboard shortcuts?

Go to chrome://extensions/shortcuts in your browser, find DialogDrive in the list, and click the pencil icon next to each command to set your preferred keyboard shortcuts.

What's the difference between popup and side panel?

Both interfaces provide the same functionality:

  • Popup: Compact view that appears when you click the extension icon. Great for quick access.
  • Side Panel: Persistent panel that stays open alongside your browsing. Better for extended workflow.

Choose whichever fits your workflow best!

Common Troubleshooting

Symptom Resolution
Popup appears blank Reload the extension from chrome://extensions. Toggle the extension off and on. Check the browser console for errors.
Keyboard shortcuts not working Verify shortcuts are configured at chrome://extensions/shortcuts. Make sure no other extension is using the same shortcut.
Data missing after update Check that the extension ID hasn't changed. Verify you're using the same browser profile. Check Chrome's storage in DevTools.
Cannot paste prompts into ChatGPT Ensure you're on chatgpt.com and the page has fully loaded. Try refreshing the ChatGPT page. Check that DialogDrive has permission to access the site.
Extension icon not appearing Click the puzzle piece icon in Chrome's toolbar and pin DialogDrive for easier access.
